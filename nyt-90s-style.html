Base Crosses $10 Billion Milestone as Ecosystem Activity Reaches New Heights
By Base Times Financial Staff
The Base blockchain network achieved a significant milestone this week as total value locked surpassed $10.2 billion, representing a 15% increase from the previous week and cementing its position as one of the fastest-growing layer-2 networks in the Ethereum ecosystem.
The surge comes amid explosive growth across multiple sectors, with decentralized finance protocols leading the charge. Aerodrome Finance, the network's flagship decentralized exchange, experienced a remarkable transformation following its Version 3 launch, which introduced concentrated liquidity pools and sparked a 20% surge in total value locked from $1.2 billion to $1.44 billion in just 48 hours.
Jesse Pollak, Base's creator and Coinbase's vice president of platform, praised the upgrade in a Twitter post, stating: "Aerodrome's upgrade is the rocket fuel Base DeFi needs—efficiency like this keeps us ahead of Solana's chaos." The platform now processes approximately $500 million in daily volume, with trading fees contributing significantly to the network's $5 million in daily revenue.
AI Integration Drives Innovation
Perhaps more striking than the financial metrics is the network's embrace of artificial intelligence technologies. The AI Agents protocol completed its migration from Ethereum mainnet to Base this week, citing an 80% reduction in gas costs as the primary motivation. The protocol now supports over 10,000 active AI agents processing approximately 500,000 tasks weekly, with total value locked in AI-related protocols reaching $200 million, up 40% from the previous period.
"Base lets AI run wild without bankruptcy," noted the protocol's founder, highlighting how the network's low transaction costs enable previously impossible use cases for autonomous agents. This development has attracted attention from institutional investors seeking exposure to the intersection of blockchain and artificial intelligence technologies.
"We're building for seamless fiat onramps—think zero-gas stables for everyday users. Base will onboard 1 billion users by year-end via AI and stables."
— Jesse Pollak, Creator of Base
Memecoin Frenzy Fuels Transaction Growth
The network has also become a hotbed for speculative trading, with memecoin activity reaching unprecedented levels. $DOGBASE, a dog-themed token launched Tuesday, surged to a $500 million market capitalization with over 100,000 holders driving 2 million daily transactions. Social media engagement data shows over 50,000 Twitter mentions, representing a 300% week-over-week increase.
However, this growth comes with challenges. Security researchers have identified over 50 suspicious Twitter accounts promoting fake $TRUST airdrop campaigns, each garnering 400-460 likes and directing users to wallet-draining websites. The campaigns specifically target Base users, leveraging the network's low fees to make fraudulent transactions economically viable.
Network statistics from L2Beat show Base now processes 50 million daily transactions, up 10% from the previous week, with 2 million daily active addresses. The network has attracted $2 billion in bridged value, with stablecoins representing 40% of total value locked, led by USDC at $4 billion.
Looking ahead, Base has confirmed its second airdrop season, targeting 500,000 eligible wallets based on DeFi activity patterns. Industry analysts expect this announcement to drive further user acquisition and ecosystem growth in the coming months.
